DisCO: Portrait Distortion Correction with Perspective-Aware 3D GANs

Portrait photos captured from a near-range distance often suffer from undesired perspective distortions. DisCO corrects these perspective distortions and synthesizes more pleasant views by virtually enlarging focal length and camera-to-subject distance.

Abstract

Close-up facial images captured at very close distances can often suffer from perspective distortion, resulting in unnatural and unattractive appearances. In this paper, we propose a new approach for correcting perspective distortions in a single close-up face using perspective-aware 3D GAN inversion. We first use pre-trained 3D GAN to perform inversion on perspective-distorted close-up facial images. Re-rendering the portrait at proper focal length and camera distance effectively correct these distortions and produce more natural-looking results. Our experiments show that our method outperforms previous approaches in terms of accuracy and visual quality. We believe it has the potential to be widely adopted in various applications.
