Abstract

Close-up facial images captured at very close distances can often suffer from perspective distortion, resulting in unnatural and unattractive appearances. In this paper, we propose a new approach for correcting perspective distortions in a single close-up face using perspective-aware 3D GAN inversion. We first use pre-trained 3D GAN to perform inversion on perspective-distorted close-up facial images. Re-rendering the portrait at proper focal length and camera distance effectively correct these distortions and produce more natural-looking results.
